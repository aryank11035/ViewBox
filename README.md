**viewBox** is a media platform where users can explore movies and shows, add favorites, and cast votes. Users can create, manage, and organize their own playlists, making it easy to keep track of what they love. The platform allows selecting content, adding it to multiple playlists, and sharing curated collections. With viewBox, discovering, organizing, and enjoying media becomes simple and interactive.
